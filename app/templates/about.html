About RemoteJobFinder

Remote work is on the rise. Companies both large and small are offering their employees the opportunity to work remotely because of the clear, measurable benefits it provides. However, the resources available to job seekers to find jobs that are 100% remote haven’t caught up to this trend. Traditional job boards aren’t built with remote work in mind, while sites focused on remote work can be filled with scams.

RemoteJobFinder is dedicated to finding real remote jobs at real companies. We start by verifying each company with postings on our site is a legitimate company with a real job to offer. No company requiring a fee to interview or an investment for their “business opportunity” will be included on the site. Additionally, we work to verify that the job can be performed remotely.

We’ve personally reviewed the careers section of hundreds of company websites to determine if they are a real remote company offering a real remote job. If the company meets our stringent requirements, we will include their openings on RemoteJobFinder.

While all of the jobs on RemoteJobFinder are remote, or home-based, some companies require applicants live in a certain geographic area, like a US state. There are several reasons why a company have this requirement. For example, companies hiring health care workers may need employees handling patient data to live in a specific state. Companies hiring sales representatives may hire remote employees that live near a segment of their customers. Others may just want their employees to be in the same time zone to ease collaboration.

Posting jobs to RemoteJobFinder is free. Submit your remote jobs here, and we’ll review your openings to determine if they meet our requirements.
